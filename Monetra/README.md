# Monetra 🌀
### Personal Finance with Premium Intelligence

Monetra is a high-end personal finance assistant designed to eliminate "math stress." Instead of just tracking expenses, Monetra uses **Daily Budgeting Logic** and **Purchase Simulation** to help you keep your savings targets secure.

---

## ✨ Core Philosophy: The Monetra Way
Most apps show you "what you spent." Monetra tells you **"what you can spend RIGHT NOW."**

1.  **Commitment First**: We take your Salary, subtract your Savings Goal, and pay your EMIs/Fixed Bills first.
2.  **Daily Guard**: The remaining money is divided by the days in the month to give you a **Safe to Spend Today** limit.
3.  **Dynamic Adjustment**: Spent too much today? Monetra automatically adjusts your limit for tomorrow to keep your month-end goal safe.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Smart Purchase Simulator (Money Coach)
Thinking of buying something? Ask the Simulator first. It uses real-time projections to give you a verdict:
*   **🟢 Safe to Buy**: Fits your disposable income perfectly.
*   **🟡 Savings Risk**: Will eat into your ₹ savings target for the month.
*   **🔴 High Impact**: Threatens your Emergency Runway or bill payments.

### 2. Weekly Budget Strategy
Stop running out of money by mid-month. The Assistant provides a **Daily Target** and **Pacing Tips** to help you stay 100% on track.

### 3. Financial Waterfall
A visual breakdown of your cash flow. Understand exactly how your income flows into Savings, Debt, and fixed commitments before it reaches your pocket.

### 4. What-If Simulator
Plan for the future. Add scenarios like "20% Salary Hike" or "New Car EMI" and see exactly how your net worth will look 12 months from now.

### 5. Premium Aesthetics
*   **Fluid Adaptive Icon**: A minimalist brand identity that adapts to your OS.
*   **Dynamic Glassmorphism**: Stunning UI with gradients and micro-animations.
*   **Dark/Light Mode Ready**: Fully optimized splash and system themes.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Language**: Kotlin
*   **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose (100% Declarative)
*   **Architecture**: Clean Architecture + MVVM + UDF (Unidirectional Data Flow)
*   **DI**: Hilt (Dagger)
*   **Database**: Room
*   **Navigation**: Type-safe Jetpack Navigation

## 📦 Installation
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open in Android Studio (Koala or later).
3. Build and Run on any Android device (API 26+).
4. **Important**: Go to Settings first to set your Monthly Salary and Savings Goal!

## 🛡 Security & Privacy
Monetra is **100% Offline**. Your financial data never leaves your device. No cloud, no tracking, no leaks.
